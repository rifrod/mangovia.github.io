Our Dessert Restaurant

Mangovia offers a fun atmosphere for lunch and dinner in a well-lit space. The menu is changed regularly to highlight the vast variety of ways mangoes can be enjoyed from around the world.

Dessert Area

Our special dessert area showcases mangoes from around the world for your enjoyment. Our recipes are world renowned. Mangovia chefs are trained by masters of many kinds. We also provide the option to enjoy seeing your dessert being made right in front of you. Which can be an experience itself. Everything that leaves our kitchen is bound to be the best tasting dessert you have experienced.
